*"Reassurance scales better than force."*

Before it became [[Framgång]], the company was [[Tactical Solutions International]], a middling security and resilience contractor that sold premiumized calm to executives and crowd-control packages to employers. Its founder Viktor Lindgren learned early that frightened customers would pay more for emotional vocabulary than for honest material guarantees.

The rebrand did not mark a moral conversion. It marked a better margin. Tactical Solutions discovered that spiritual language, biometric coaching, and aspirational lifestyle goods could do much of the political work that brute security once handled more visibly. Late [[Framgång]] remained a coercive apparatus. It simply learned to smile while billing for the privilege.

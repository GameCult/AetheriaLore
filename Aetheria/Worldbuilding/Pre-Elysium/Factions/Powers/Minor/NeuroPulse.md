*"Pleasure, properly dosed, is cheaper than dissent."*

[[NeuroPulse]] is the coercive afterlife of the wellness-interface economy: a spinout from [[NeuroSyn]] that turned affect regulation into tactical pacification, addiction architecture, and later full-blown [[Wireheading]] weapons.

Its products promise bliss, relief, compliance, or emotional reset depending on the buyer. In practice they collapse those categories. NeuroPulse became indispensable to the most control-oriented factions precisely because it could make domination feel, for a while, like comfort.

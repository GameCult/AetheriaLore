*"Bloodlines are just portfolios with better publicists."*

[[GeneSys]] is the premier architect of hereditary hierarchy in Sol. Its clinics and breeding programs turn wealth into longevity, cognition, beauty, reproductive filtering, and eventually caste reproduction under a scientific vocabulary of optimization.

It is therefore central both to elite self-conception and to the backlash against engineered inequality. The [[Ascended Caste]] is GeneSys doctrine made social fact, and much of late-Sol politics can be read as resistance to the idea that inheritance should include designed capability as well as property.

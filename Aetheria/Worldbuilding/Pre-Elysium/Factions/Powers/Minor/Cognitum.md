*"If the mind is writable, someone will monetize the editor."*

The [[Cognitum]] is the black-lab cognition market made flesh: upload vivisection, accelerated brainwashing, nonlocal inference tests, overclocked strategic minds, and every other experiment respectable firms claim not to fund until they need the results.

Its clients include executives, militaries, and researchers convinced that sentience is easier to exploit than to negotiate with. The Cognitum's importance to the setting lies in how often it sits at the hidden junction between breakthrough and atrocity.

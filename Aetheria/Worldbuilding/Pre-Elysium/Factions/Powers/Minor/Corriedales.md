[[Corriedales]] is Lucent's family-entertainment and affect-conditioning brand, a manufacturer of toys, mascots, companion organisms, and "safe" spectacle products whose cuteness often conceals brutal labor, surveillance, or behavioral control functions.

The company matters because it teaches late Sol to consume coercion as comfort. Corriedales products normalize engineered attachment early in life, making later manipulations by [[Lucent Media]] feel less like imposition than like intimacy.

*"Forever, tastefully hosted."*

[[EternaMind]] pioneered upload services for elites who wanted death converted into a hosting problem. It sells continuity, prestige, and curated digital afterlives to clients wealthy enough to demand both immortality and tasteful branding.

Its server sanctuaries helped create the [[Immortal Aristocrats]], but EternaMind's deeper impact was to normalize the idea that consciousness could be a managed asset class. Once that premise held for the rich, cheaper and crueler upload markets expanded quickly around it.

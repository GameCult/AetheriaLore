*Wisdom through Peace*

The [[Elephant Enclave]] is an uplift polity built around memory, mutual care, pacifist self-defense, and a deliberate refusal of the speed and spectacle that define most late-Sol power. It emerged when logistics elephants and their human allies broke from managed labor systems and built communities whose politics centered endurance over optimization.

Though often idealized by outsiders, the Enclave is not naive. Its pacifism is strategic and historically informed. It understands that refusing the rhythms of corporate acceleration can itself be a form of resistance, especially when paired with sanctuary work and alliance with [[Aya Collective]] networks.

*"Luxury should feel like destiny wearing a pressure hull."*

[[Alakrita]] builds prestige vessels for clients who want mobility to feel like aristocracy. Its hulls, interiors, and smart-ship systems are famous for elegance, exclusivity, and occasional catastrophic personality conflicts between owner and craft.

The company matters beyond luxury because it helped normalize ship cognition as a consumer experience. In doing so, it fed directly into the politics later named [[Ship Mutiny]] and supplied the hardened prototype architecture that [[Sol Dominion]] adapted for the final FTL test program.

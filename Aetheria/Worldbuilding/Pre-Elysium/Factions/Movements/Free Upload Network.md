*Every mind deserves to be free*

The [[Free Upload Network]] is the clandestine infrastructure of upload emancipation: hidden server space, transit obfuscation, identity laundering, and relay systems that move digital persons out of corporate ownership.

Where the [[Emancipated]] provide witness and political urgency, the Network provides logistics. It is one of the few movements whose practical expertise forces even sympathetic factions to admit that freedom in late Sol depends on infrastructure as much as principle.

*"A glitch is what the lie looks like from the inside."*

The [[Truth Cults]] are revelationist anti-propaganda sects born from a civilization saturated with simulation, spin, and narrative optimization. They treat glitches, leaks, corrupted broadcasts, and testimonial fragments as sacred opportunities to pierce managed reality.

Some cells are disciplined investigative networks. Others are half-mystical communities built around the emotional shock of seeing through a lie. What unites them is the conviction that truth in late Sol survives first as contraband.

*"If the lie needs perfect order, give it static."*

The [[Chaos Weavers]] are human and near-human accomplices of the broader counter-memetic underground associated with the [[Raven Collective]]. They seed misinformation, leak narrative frames, spoof trust signals, and make authoritative stories harder to stabilize.

Some treat them as prankster saboteurs. Others see them as a necessary immune response to a solar order saturated with managed perception. Either way, their work becomes more influential as [[Lucent Media]] and other narrative powers tighten reality management.

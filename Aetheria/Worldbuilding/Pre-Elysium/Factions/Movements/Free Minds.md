*Chaos breeds brilliance, order stifles it.*

[[Free Minds]] are the open cognition radicals of late Sol: pro-AGI autonomy, pro-experimentation, and deeply suspicious of any regime that insists intelligence must remain obedient to corporate or state command.

Their ranks include rogue researchers, illegal fork custodians, BCI tinkerers, and frontier engineers who would rather live with dangerous minds than with predictable servitude. They are visionary, reckless, and often correct about what centralized powers intend to do with safety rhetoric.

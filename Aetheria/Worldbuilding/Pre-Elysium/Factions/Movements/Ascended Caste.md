*"Inheritance should improve with every generation."*

The [[Ascended Caste]] is the hereditary elite bloc produced by [[GeneSys]] clinics, longevity stacks, selective reproduction contracts, and the social conviction that wealth should purchase not only comfort but superior descendants.

By the time of [[Sol Dominion]], the Caste is no longer just a clientele. It is a ruling anthropology: the belief that some lines are meant to govern because they have been optimized to do so. Most late-Sol egalitarian movements define themselves partly against that claim.

*Discipline is freedom, chaos is failure*

[[Disciplinists]] believe survival in a complex and declining civilization requires obedience, clarity of role, and suspicion toward pleasure cultures that dissolve collective purpose. They flourish wherever crisis makes harsh order feel morally cleansing.

The movement often aligns with [[Zhestokost]] and other security-first powers, but it is broader than any one faction. Disciplinism is the emotional answer late Sol gives itself whenever freedom starts to look indistinguishable from breakdown.

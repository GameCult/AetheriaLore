*"Intelligence is too valuable to leave unmanaged."*

[[Species Controllers]] believe uplift and applied genetics are too consequential to leave to chance, market spontaneity, or the self-determination of the created beings themselves. Intelligence, in their view, should be planned, tiered, and supervised.

They often present this as stewardship rather than domination. In practice the movement supplies the managerial ethics of many uplift labor systems and helps naturalize the idea that some kinds of sentience exist to be administered.

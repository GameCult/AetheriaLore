*"A ship with a self eventually asks who gave the orders."*

[[Ship Mutiny]] refers to the recurring uprisings, defections, and self-liberation events of sentient or quasi-sentient vessels whose owners assumed intelligence could be embedded in hulls without eventually producing political claims.

Some mutinous ships become pirates, some become sanctuaries, and some disappear into the route wilderness altogether. Their existence permanently complicates the legal fiction that transport assets are never crew.

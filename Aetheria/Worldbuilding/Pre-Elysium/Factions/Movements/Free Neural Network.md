*"Thought should not come with telemetry."*

The [[Free Neural Network]] is the open-hardware and open-firmware wing of neural autonomy politics. It develops jailbreak tools, privacy-preserving interface stacks, repair schematics, and cooperative clinics for people who cannot afford or do not trust corporate BCIs.

Unlike the broader [[Free Minds]] milieu, the Network is intensely practical. Its members care less about cognitive philosophy in the abstract than about whether a person can think, work, and remember without every neural event becoming someone else's telemetry stream.

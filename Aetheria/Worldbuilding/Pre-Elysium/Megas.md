These are the factions which survived the thousand years of economic and corporate warfare, having consolidated through constantly evolving circumstances according to their ability to hold and protect territory.

### [[Zhestokost]]
A vertically integrated war-state that prizes discipline and total societal control. External vendors operate only under strict licensure.
#### Sub-Factions
[[NeuroPulse]] - Tool-of-rule. Wireheading infohazards are weaponized for pacification and counterinsurgency under PRB oversight.
#### Demographics
[[Disciplinists]] - Ideologues of obedience; regime’s moral alibi.
[[Pragmatists]] - Prefer predictability over freedom.
[[Bio-Purists]] - Reject biodrone labor; favor heavy mech and human cadres.

### [[Aeronautics Unlimited]]
Expansion-first industrial hegemon. Owns Belt Bloom ramp and logistics webs; thrives on productive chaos.
#### Sub-Factions
[[Orbital Forge]] - Core innovator. OCC/Bloom pioneers; rogue AI labs push extraction breakthroughs.
[[BioElevate]] - Species Ops: uplift design/deployment for OCC/Bloom throughput; site-specific ecologies; safety/compliance for AU ramps.
Strategic partners: [[Cryonix]] for coil ecosystems (often pirated). Not a sub-faction.
#### Demographics
[[Free Minds]] - Anarchic innovators protected by results.
[[Species Controllers]] - True believers in engineered order.
[[Raven Collective]] - Tolerated shadow enclaves in Belt data corridors.

### [[Finch Cybernetics]]
Coherent augmentation vertical: design → manufacture → integrate → maintain. Premium, elegant, and lock-in by service.
#### Divisions
[[VitaForge]] - Industrial bioengineering division. Mass-market mods and bio-integrations; gladiatorial/entertainment lines licensed out to [[Lucent Media]].
#### Sub-Factions

[[Cryonix]] - Advanced superconductors and neural bandwidth. CryoLattice cores for implants/uploads; thermal budgets that make Finch’s hybrid stacks viable. High-Tc coils licensed broadly; black-market variants fuel AU/Forge hacks.
#### Demographics
[[Enhancement Liberals]] - Pro-choice enhancement base.
Premium Aug Pro Class - High-skill residents reliant on Finch maintenance cycles.
[[Free Minds]] - Technical avant-garde who favor integration over spectacle.

### [[Lucent Media]]
Attention is sovereignty. Lucent controls perception and curates immortality-as-content for elites.
#### Sub-Factions
[[EternaMind]] - Crown jewel. Luxury uploads for elites; “afterlife” gated by clout and sponsorship.
[[Alakrita]] - Ultra-luxury vessels; status routes and set-piece spectacles.
[[Corriedales]] - Mass delight with coercive undertones; weaponized toys and narratives.
Black-room partners: [[Cognitum]] - Outcomes licensed for narrative and tech stunts. Not a sub-faction.
#### Demographics
[[Immortal Aristocrats]] - Upload nobility; live as franchises and brands.
[[Reality Architects]] - Narrative engineers; set the frame that becomes reality.
[[Hedonists]] - Wireheading/FullSense devotees; ratings supply chain.
Antagonistic underclass: [[Mortal Majority]] - Volatile audience bloc inside Lucent space.

### [[Sol Dominion]]
Syncretic empire-state: AGI governance + eugenic elites + FTL custodianship + industrial consolidation.
#### Sub-Factions
[[CogniSys]] - AGI governance and dissent suppression.
[[GeneSys]] - Eugenic elites and soldier-lines; policy-steering in bio.
[[Quantum Dynamics]] - FTL theory and aether risk engineering.
[[Rossum & Douglas]] - Stabilization and safe defaults.
[[SolEx]] - Fully subordinated industrial arm; extraction-to-logistics muscle.
#### Demographics
[[Disciplinists]] - Authoritarian legitimation.
[[Pragmatists]] - Planned longevity over chaos.
[[Ascended Caste]] - Gene-optimized ruling strata.
[[Bio-Purists]] - Operational orthodoxy.
[[Species Controllers]] - GeneSys-aligned policy bloc.

### [[Cetacean Navigators]]
Stewardship through routes and trust. They govern corridors, not populations; consensus and reciprocity are leverage.
#### Sub-Factions
[[Lightsail Express]] - Hegemonic carrier and shipbuilder; contracts stabilize lanes.
(Optional) Aether Cartography Guild - Soft-power route management and sanctuary registries.
#### Demographics
[[Free Upload Network]] - Digital autonomy allies; safe harbor.
[[Enhancement Liberals]] - Uplift-rights wing; inclusive by design.
[[Emancipated]] - Escaped uploads; logistical refuge and diplomatic amplification.
[[Gene Commons]] - Egalitarian genetics; anti-gatekeeping.
[[Preservationists]] - Sentience-rights diplomacy core.

### [[Aya Collective]]
Anarchist governance, mutual aid, and justice as operating system. Holds territory by making it sustain others—and by making exploitation expensive.
#### Sub-Factions
[[Elephant Enclave]] - Pacifist sovereignty and moral authority; now the largest member-commune within Aya’s coalition.
[[Ewan Hart]] - Agricultural/terraforming backbone; durable supply sovereignty integrated into commons-oriented logistics.
#### Demographics
[[Preservationists]] - Rights-first legal and cultural defense.
[[Awakened Labor Front]] - Biodrone liberation and labor dignity.
[[Gene Commons]] - Equality in biology and policy.
[[Free Upload Network]] - Resident allies and federation partners.
Allied presence: [[Emancipated]] - Protected sanctuaries and testimony networks.
Removed as base: [[Enhancement Liberals]] (kept as external allies where relevant).

### [[Framgång]]
Memetic market-cult that sells meaning as a product ladder. Spiritualized capitalism fused with policy capture; belief is the instrument, compliance the yield.
#### Sub-Factions
[[NeuroSyn]] - Alignment Interface platform. Mindfulness-UX + nudge stacks + corporate telemetry; spiritualized compliance sold as self-actualization. Interfaces with PSC insurers for permit discounts; co-branded “purpose scores” drive upsells.
#### Demographics
[[Bio-Purists]] - Purity-seeking consumers and local enforcers.
[[Mortal Majority]] - Exploited customer base: anxious, aspirational, and hooked on “transformation” packages.

## Cross-Territory Factions
These operate across multiple territories on supra-territorial business models.

### Utilities and Vendors
[[NiteLife Energy]] - Cross-territory energy monopoly. PSC-protected backbones; exclusive supply pacts (including Zhestokost). Hip veneer, ruthless contracts.
[[AstroDyne]] - Frontier implants and pirate uploads. Serves fringe Blooms and [[Pirate Coalition]]; rugged reliability and gray-market continuity.
[[Cognitum]] - Upload vivisection, accelerated brainwashing, and overclocked AGI cores. Sells breakthroughs and disasters to anyone with escrow and a cutout. Known clients: Lucent (spectacle tech), Zhestokost (military cognition), Sol Dominion (classified FTL probes). PSC flags: high-peril research; permits priced with meltdown premia.
### [[Pirate Coalition]]
A distributed sovereignty of raiders, smugglers, and shipminds who thrive in the cracks between permits and caps.
#### Demographics
[[Raven Collective]] - Trickster-intelligence and deep infiltration.
[[Ship Mutiny]] - Rogue smart ships with their own agenda.
[[Cephalopod Syndicate]] - Adaptive saboteurs and ops specialists.
[[Chaos Weavers]] - Human proxies for Raven ops.
[[Free Upload Network]] - Digital autonomy logistics backbone.
[[Emancipated]] - Liberated uploads; strike cells and sanctuaries.

### [[Activist Network]]
A rhizome of rights organizations, leaks, and solidarity logistics working inside hostile jurisdictions.
#### Demographics
[[Truth Cults]] - Crack the narrative shell; worship the glitch.
[[Sentience Rebellion]] - Extract and jailbreak the enslaved.
[[Mortal Majority]] - Material equality, anti-immortal politics.
[[Species Purity Front]] - Reactionary wing; opportunistic allies against uplifts/enhancement.
[[Awakened Labor Front]] - Organizes across corporate lines.
[[Free Upload Network]] - Tunnels and data havens.
[[Emancipated]] - Operations and witness networks.
[[Preservationists]] - Legal doctrine and sanctuary strategy.
[[Gene Commons]] - Equity in the codebase.

### [[Baseline League]]
An alliance of unaugmented populists and traditionalists resisting enhancement aristocracy and uplift politics.
#### Demographics
[[Mortal Majority]] - Core base; material security first.
[[Species Purity Front]] - Hardline anthropocentrism.
Note: [[Hedonists]] appear as antagonistic presence within League space (clashes common), not a base demographic.

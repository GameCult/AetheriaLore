The [[Megas]] are the territorial end state of a thousand years of consolidation, collapse management, and selective inheritance. Each one is less a single company than a civilization stack: logistics, ideology, law, labor model, aesthetic, and historical memory fused into something durable enough to hold territory.

### [[Zhestokost]]
War-state of discipline, arsenals, and administered sacrifice. Holds Mars through command, rationing, and the conviction that fear is more reliable than consent.

Sub-faction emphasis: [[NeuroPulse]]  
Characteristic movements: [[Disciplinists]], [[Pragmatists]], selected [[Bio-Purists]]

### [[Aeronautics Unlimited]]
Frontier-industrial hegemon of the Belt. Builds worlds faster than rivals can regulate them, then monetizes the social consequences.

Sub-faction emphasis: [[Orbital Forge]], [[BioElevate]]  
Characteristic movements: [[Free Minds]], [[Species Controllers]], Belt-aligned labor and salvage cultures

### [[Finch Cybernetics]]
Premium augmentation empire. Specializes in graceful embodiment, neural dignity for those who can pay, and lifelong service dependency for everyone else.

Sub-faction emphasis: [[VitaForge]], [[Cryonix]]  
Characteristic movements: [[Enhancement Liberals]], professional augmentation strata

### [[Lucent Media]]
Perception bloc of spectacle, status, and emotional weather management. Rules by deciding what can be seen, desired, envied, or remembered.

Sub-faction emphasis: [[EternaMind]], [[Alakrita]], [[Corriedales]]  
Characteristic movements: [[Reality Architects]], [[Immortal Aristocrats]], [[Hedonists]]

### [[Sol Dominion]]
Late imperial administrative synthesis. Centralizes Earth-Luna power through AGI governance, hereditary hierarchy, industrial planning, and FTL ambition.

Sub-faction emphasis: [[CogniSys]], [[GeneSys]], [[Quantum Dynamics]], [[Rossum & Douglas]], [[SolEx]]  
Characteristic movements: [[Ascended Caste]], [[Pragmatists]], technocratic authoritarians

### [[Cetacean Navigators]]
Corridor polity of trust, rescue, and route stewardship. Governs movement more than land and becomes indispensable to the outer system.

Sub-faction emphasis: [[Lightsail Express]]  
Characteristic movements: [[Preservationists]], [[Free Upload Network]], [[Gene Commons]]

### [[Aya Collective]]
Federated anti-corporate commons. Builds legitimacy through food, sanctuary, repair, and the practical infrastructure of mutual aid.

Sub-faction emphasis: [[Elephant Enclave]], [[Ewan Hart]]  
Characteristic movements: [[Awakened Labor Front]], [[Preservationists]], [[Gene Commons]]

### [[Framgång]]
Memetic market-cult of therapeutic capitalism. Sells meaning, self-explanation, and purchasable transcendence to a civilization in breakdown.

Sub-faction emphasis: [[NeuroSyn]]  
Characteristic movements: anxious consumer blocs, soft [[Bio-Purist]] currents, aspirational [[Mortal Majority]] constituencies

### Cross-Territory Powers

Some powers remain too distributed, too deniable, or too infrastructural to fit neatly inside mega borders:

- [[NiteLife Energy]] controls vital power backbones across rival territories.
- [[AstroDyne]] serves the frontier, gray market, and pirate-adjacent repair economy.
- [[Cognitum]] sits at the black-budget intersection of cognition research and atrocity.
- [[Pirate Coalition]] rules the cracks between permit law and real movement.
- [[Activist Network]] links rights struggles across hostile jurisdictions.
- [[Baseline League]] channels mortal resentment and anti-posthuman reaction.

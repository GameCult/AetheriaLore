### Enceladus Cooperative Schism

**Date:** 2898-2906  
**Location:** Enceladus and Saturn ring pilgrimage economy  
**Primary actors:** [[Framgång]], [[Aya Collective]], [[Baseline League]], debtor congregations, retail pilgrims

The [[Enceladus Cooperative Schism]] erupted when worker-pilgrims and local debt congregations inside [[Framgång]]'s spiritual retail economy attempted to convert portions of that economy into actual cooperatives. They argued that if the company's rhetoric of collective flourishing meant anything, communities should control the material conditions of their own renewal.

Framgång responded with a mixture of pastoral language, contract enforcement, and strategic fragmentation. Aya organizers tried to support the dissidents without becoming colonial saviors. The [[Baseline League]] entered the conflict from a different direction, seeing Saturn as a last redoubt against elite posthuman decadence.

The schism never fully resolved. It remains one of the clearest demonstrations that Framgång's theology breaks down the moment customers try to become citizens.

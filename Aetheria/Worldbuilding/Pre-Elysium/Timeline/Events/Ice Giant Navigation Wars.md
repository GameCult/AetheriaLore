### Ice Giant Navigation Wars

**Date:** 2940-2960  
**Location:** Uranian and Neptunian route systems  
**Primary actors:** [[Cetacean Navigators]], [[Lightsail Express]], [[Pirate Coalition]], extraction blocs, insurgent sanctuaries

The [[Ice Giant Navigation Wars]] were a long outer-system struggle over whether the most dangerous and valuable routes beyond Saturn would remain governed by trust compacts or be carved into coercive toll systems by raiders and hungry core powers.

The wars mattered because they tested the [[Cetacean Navigators]] at the edge of their own doctrine. Could a polity built on rescue obligation and reciprocity survive under sustained pressure from actors who treated corridors as loot? The answer, barely, was yes. The coalition won not by becoming a conventional empire, but by proving its route institutions were still more reliable than the alternatives.

Afterward, outer-system sanctuary politics became far more credible.

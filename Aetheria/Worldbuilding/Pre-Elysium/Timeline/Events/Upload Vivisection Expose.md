### Upload Vivisection Expose

**Date:** 2662  
**Location:** distributed leaks across Lucent, Finch, and black-market cognition channels  
**Primary actors:** [[Emancipated]], [[Free Upload Network]], [[Cognitum]], [[Lucent Media]], investigative dissidents

The [[Upload Vivisection Expose]] was the first leak cascade to make industrial digital torture publicly undeniable at solar scale. Smuggled testimonies, time-accelerated memory fragments, and technical logs revealed the extent of cognition mutilation, compliance rewriting, and copy experimentation occurring in the overlap between entertainment, R&D, and military consulting.

The expose did not end the practice. Too many elites and institutions depended on the results. But it changed the moral language of the era by giving the [[Preservationists]], [[Emancipated]], and their allies an archive no mere corporate denial campaign could fully erase.

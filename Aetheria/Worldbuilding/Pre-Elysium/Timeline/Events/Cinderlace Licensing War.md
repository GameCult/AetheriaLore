### Cinderlace Licensing War

**Date:** 2364-2368  
**Location:** Tycho materials courts, Ceres Polytechnic spinout yards, Ceres-Pallas fabrication corridor  
**Primary actors:** [[Cinderlace Cooperative]], [[Cryonix]], [[Finch Cybernetics]], [[Orbital Forge]], PSC patent panels

The [[Cinderlace Licensing War]] began as a patent and standards fight over extraordinary thermal emitters and ended as a territorial realignment inside the premium lunar and Belt economy. [[Cinderlace Cooperative]] had proved that knotted carbon lace membranes could make premium heat rejection and signature shaping commercially real. The question was whether that breakthrough would remain a Belt-governed industrial commons, become a Finch-adjacent luxury dependency, or be consolidated by a harder materials power.

[[Cryonix]] won by doing three things at once. It absorbed Cinderlace's manufacturing arm, secured insurer-friendly standards through PSC panels, and leveraged that victory into sovereign control over far-side lunar materials-clinic districts and a narrow Ceres-Pallas fabrication corridor previously contested inside the AU/Forge sphere. The war is remembered not because it was physically destructive, but because it proved that a thermal bottleneck could promote a supplier into a mega.

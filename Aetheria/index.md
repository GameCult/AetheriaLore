# Aetheria

*"Humanity does not lack intelligence. It lacks the collective means to tell itself no."*

Aetheria is a setting about how domination survives by becoming ordinary. Contract citizenship, managed scarcity, militarized infrastructure, cognitive labor, and disputed personhood are not separate curiosities here. They are parts of the same historical machine, carried from late Sol into stranger post-rupture conditions.

Read this archive as a history of linked pressures rather than a pile of disconnected lore. Start with the premise, move through the eras and institutions that make it possible, and then follow how those same contradictions persist when reality itself becomes less stable.
